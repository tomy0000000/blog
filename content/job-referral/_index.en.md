If you're looking for a referral to Robinhood, I'm genuinely happy to help! But do read through this first, it'll save us both time, and set you up for the best shot.

## 👀 Step 1: Find the _right_ opening

All public roles are on [job board](https://boards.greenhouse.io/embed/job_board?for=Robinhood). Those are the only ones I can refer you to.

Before you reach out, be honest with yourself:

- Do you fit the role description by at least 75%?
- Do you meet the job level / years of experience requirement?

{{< alert "circle-info" >}}
Even when YoE isn't explicitly stated, the salary range is a reliable signal. A starting range of $166,000 for a general software engineer in Zone 1 typically implies 2–3 years of experience. If you're a new grad with less than 1 YoE, make sure you have a compelling narrative to bridge that gap.
{{< /alert >}}

I won't check if you tick all the boxes, my stance is to refer broadly and let the recruiters do their job. That said, a level mismatch without a strong justification gets filtered out in seconds. Be strategic.

{{< alert "circle-info" >}}

**Nothing suitable right now?**

New roles drop regularly. Set up a job alert on Greenhouse, and circle back when the right one appears.

It's far better to show up as a standout candidate at the right moment than to fire off applications all year and be background noise when it counts.

{{< /alert >}}

```
- *Email
- Phone

- Website
- *LinkedIn

```

This inbox is _exclusively_ for referral requests. Please don't use it for anything else, or you'll be blocked.

Remember to double-check that your resume is attached. You'd be surprised. ;-)

## ✅ Step 3: Watch for confirmation

I send referrals in batches on most workdays. Once yours goes through, you'll receive a confirmation email from Greenhouse with further instructions. That confirmation means you're in the pipeline. Our recruiters will reach out directly if your profile catches their eye.

No word after 3 days? Feel free to nudge me.
